SFX Narrative

Created Fall 2023 by Samuel Villalobos

Requirements for Project: The student will create a short audio-only narrative occurring in a specific environment. The narrative will be expressed by layering sound effects together to tell the story and define the environment. Project should be at least 1 minute in length and use at least 10 separate sound effects.

Read description after hearing the SFX narrative.

Description: A narrative about someone walking away from a group of people and getting a call from a seriel killer which he ignores. He then proceeds to hear the same melody from the phone in real life which scares him, and he calls 911, and in the process the killer stabs the victim, and we hear his last heartbeat.

References and Credits:

  • The Whistle of Death was used from the movie Puss in Boots 2

Audio Story

Created Spring 2024 by Samuel Villalobos

Requirements for Project: Produce an audio story approximately 90 seconds in length. Write the dialog for the story, record narration/voice-overs, apply sound effects, and score the piece with music edited to fit the structure of the overall project. All elements will be layered together in a multi-track environment.

Description: A story about brainstorming for an audio story project where I layer sound effects to what I am saying during the story. At the end, I break the fourth wall, and a misterious person tells me to go back to telling the story which I comically notice late that there was another person there.

Side note: I played the piano for this Audio Story.

Simple Underscoring

Created Fall 2023 by Samuel Villalobos

The underscoring is organize with the following: Underscoring rain, Underscoring Version 1, and Underscoring Version 2

Requirements for Project: The student will use recorded dialog and underscore it 3 different ways: two with different clips of music, and once using an environmental ambient sound effect. The dialog track should be approximately 30 seconds long, but can be a bit longer or shorter as needed by the dialog to make sense.

References and Credits:

  • Dialog was taken from Searra Sawka'sYoutube channel
  • Version 1 uses Code Vein's Memory of the Lost
  • Version 2 uses a Lo-Fi version of The Legend of Zelda's Fairy Fountain

Simple Recomposition

Created Fall 2023 by Samuel Villalobos

Requirements for Project: The student will pick a song over 5 minutes in length and restructure (edit) it down to less than 2 minutes. The goal is to make the edited song sound as if it were originally composed in its shortened form. Focus will be on the structure of the new composition and quality of editing.

Description: I made a seemless shorter version of Just the Two of Us

Animation Soundscape

Created Spring 2024 by Samuel Villalobos

Requirements for Project: Choose a short, animated clip approximately 45 seconds in length with no dialog. Build a soundscape from ambient and environmental sounds that support the visual space. Add specific sound effects to reinforce any physical action seen or implied in the animated work. Create a simple original ambient score that provides an emotional foundation to the animation.

References and Credits:

  • I used the Six-Tails Naruto vs Pain | Naruto Shippuden fight and scraped all the audio of the video, and I made the whole soundscape for the characters and environment
